Mission

Promote safe, structured, competitive kickboxing across Thane.

From first-time youth participants to academy athletes preparing for state-level competition, the association exists to create clarity, opportunity, and credibility around the sport.

The website supports that mission with fast access to event dates, leadership details, gallery highlights, and athlete-facing communication.

Recognized effort and visible progress
